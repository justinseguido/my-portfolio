01 Speed to Lead

Instant response within 60 seconds of form submission. First responder wins 78% of the time.

TriggerNew lead submits form, ad, or chat widget
Instant · 0 secAuto-SMS: "Thanks [Name]! We got your request for [Service]. Quick question…"
+30 secAuto-Email: Welcome + service menu + booking link
+60 secInternal alert to front desk: "New lead — [Name] — [Service Interest]"
DecisionLead replies or books within 10 min?
YES
→ Move to Booking workflow
NO
→ Drip sequence: Day 1, 3, 7 follow-ups

02 Booking Confirmation

Appointment booked → confirm + prep the client before they walk in.

TriggerAppointment booked via calendar link or manual entry
InstantSMS + Email confirmation with date, time, location, parking
ActionAdd to pipeline: "Appointment Booked" stage
+1 hourSend intake form / consent docs / pre-care instructions
ResultClient arrives prepped → smoother visit, higher conversion to treatment

03 Appointment Reminders

Multi-touch reminders that reduce no-shows by up to 40%.

TriggerAppointment exists in calendar (any status)
48 hours beforeSMS: "Reminder — your appointment is in 2 days. Reply C to confirm."
24 hours beforeEmail reminder with pre-care checklist + directions
2 hours beforeFinal SMS: "See you today at [Time]! Reply R to reschedule."
DecisionClient confirms or reschedules?
CONFIRM
Tag: Confirmed ✓
RESCHED

Automated win-back sequence when a client misses their appointment.

TriggerAppointment status → No Show (manual or auto-detected)
+30 minSMS: "We missed you today! Life happens — here's a link to rebook."
+24 hoursEmail: Rebook link + limited-time rebooking incentive
+72 hoursFinal SMS follow-up with direct booking link
DecisionRebooks within 7 days?
YES
→ Back to Booking flow
NO
Tag: No Show · Move to Reactivation

05 New Client Onboarding

Post-first-visit nurture to convert one-time visitors into repeat clients.

TriggerFirst appointment completed (status: Showed / Completed)
+1 hourThank you SMS + post-care instructions for treatment received
+24 hoursEmail: "How are you feeling?" check-in + aftercare tips
Day 7Email: Recommended next treatment + membership/package offer
Day 14SMS: "Ready for your next visit? Book now" + calendar link
ResultNew client converts to repeat client → enters Review Request flow

YES

NO
