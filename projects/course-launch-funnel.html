Overview

A launch system that fills seats and closes sales.

This coaching brand had a proven program and loyal audience, but their launches were chaotic — manually emailing a list, posting on social media, and hoping enough people would sign up. There was no funnel, no structured email sequence, and no way to track where registrants dropped off.

The project was a complete launch system: ads driving traffic to a webinar registration funnel, an automated reminder sequence to maximize show-up rates, a 3-day cart-open email series creating urgency, and a sales page designed to convert — all wired together inside GoHighLevel.

The result is a repeatable launch machine that the coach can run every quarter with predictable results instead of last-minute scrambling.

The Challenge

Launches were manual, messy, and unpredictable.

The coaching brand had strong content and a growing audience, but every launch felt like starting from scratch. No registration funnel, no automated email sequences, no show-up reminders, and no structured sales page. The result was low webinar attendance, weak conversions, and a coach burned out from doing everything manually.

  • No webinar registration funnel — signups came from scattered social media posts
  • No reminder sequence — only 20-30% of registrants actually showed up live
  • Manual email blasts instead of structured launch sequences with urgency
  • No sales page — course details were shared in a Google Doc link
  • No ad campaigns — launches relied entirely on organic reach from existing audience
  • No tracking — impossible to know where registrants dropped off or what converted
The Solution

A repeatable launch system from ad to enrollment.

01

Webinar Registration Funnel

High-converting registration page with social proof, countdown timer, and 2-step opt-in. Designed to maximize sign-ups from both paid and organic traffic.

02

Show-Up Sequence

4-email + SMS reminder sequence at registration, 48h, 24h, and 1h before the webinar — including value teasers and calendar links to maximize live attendance.

03

Sales Page

Long-form sales page with modules breakdown, testimonials, FAQ section, pricing with payment plans, and countdown timer — built for conversions, not just information.

04

Cart-Open Email Series

3-day urgency-driven email sequence: Day 1 (doors open + early bird bonus), Day 2 (case study + objection handling), Day 3 (final call + countdown). Each email drives to the sales page.

05

Ad Campaign Setup

Facebook and Google Ads campaigns targeting cold audiences with interest-based targeting, plus retargeting campaigns for webinar registrants who didn't purchase. Full pixel and conversion tracking.

06

Post-Purchase Onboarding

Welcome sequence for new students with login details, community access, first-week roadmap, and a check-in email at Day 7 — reducing refund requests and increasing completion rates.

Key Features

What makes this launch system repeatable.

Webinar Replay Funnel

Automated replay page goes live after the webinar ends, with its own email sequence for registrants who missed the live event.

Countdown Urgency

Dynamic countdown timers on the sales page and in emails — synced to the actual cart-close deadline for authentic urgency.

Launch Dashboard

Real-time pipeline showing registrants, attendees, sales page visitors, and buyers — with conversion rates at every stage for data-driven optimization.

🎯

Multi-Platform Ads

Facebook and Google Ads driving to registration, with retargeting audiences auto-built from funnel visitors and email engagers.

🔁

Smart Triggers

Workflows fire based on real-time events — registration, webinar attendance, page visits, email clicks, and purchases — not manual button clicks.
