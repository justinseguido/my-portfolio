Overview

Instant lead assignment that prevents lead loss.

Most businesses lose leads not because of bad marketing, but because of slow response times and manual processes. When a new lead comes in, it sits in a queue until someone manually checks, assigns it, and follows up — by then, the lead has already moved on.

This system solves that by automating the entire lead routing process inside GoHighLevel. From the moment a lead enters the CRM, it’s automatically tagged, scored, assigned to the right team member via round-robin, and placed into a follow-up sequence — all within seconds.

The result is zero leads lost, faster response times, and a pipeline that moves itself forward without manual intervention.

The Challenge

Leads were falling through the cracks at every step.

The team was generating leads from multiple sources — ads, forms, referrals — but had no system to route them quickly. Manual assignment meant delays, missed follow-ups, and zero visibility into who was handling what.

  • New leads sat unassigned for hours or days before anyone reached out
  • No round-robin system — some agents overloaded, others with empty pipelines
  • No lead scoring — hot leads treated the same as cold inquiries
  • Follow-up was manual and inconsistent across the team
  • No pipeline visibility — management couldn't track lead status or agent performance
  • Stale leads piled up with no automated re-engagement system
The Solution

6 workflows. Instant assignment.

01

Lead Capture & Intake

Centralized intake from all channels — forms, ads, chat, and manual entry — into one CRM with automatic source tagging and UTM tracking.

02

Instant Lead Assignment

Round-robin routing assigns each lead to the next available agent within seconds, with instant SMS and email alerts to the assigned team member.

03

Lead Qualification

Automated scoring based on lead source, form data, and engagement level — hot leads get priority alerts, warm leads enter nurture, cold leads get long-term drip.

04

Automated Follow-up

Multi-channel drip sequences via SMS and email on Days 1, 2, 4, and 7 — personalized messaging that keeps leads warm without manual effort.

05

Pipeline Management

Visual pipeline with automated stage movement based on real actions — replies, calls, bookings — plus stale lead alerts and daily summary reports.

06

Appointment & Handoff

Qualified leads are guided to book with automated confirmations, multi-touch reminders at 24h and 2h, and no-show recovery sequences built in.
