9 domains, one seamless workspace.

A client needed Google Workspace configured across 9 separate domains — each requiring DNS record updates, TXT verification, and proper email routing through Namecheap’s DNS management panel.

They had initially hired another provider to handle the setup, but the job stalled. The previous provider couldn’t complete the DNS configurations correctly, leaving domains unverified and email routing broken.

The Challenge

Previous provider couldn't deliver.

The client had already paid another provider to configure Google Workspace across their 9 domains. The job was left incomplete — DNS records were misconfigured, domains were unverified, and email wasn’t routing properly. They needed someone who could take over and get it done right.

  • 9 domains required individual DNS/TXT verification through Namecheap
  • Previous provider left the setup incomplete with misconfigured records
  • MX records were missing or incorrect — email wasn’t routing to Google Workspace
  • No SPF, DKIM, or DMARC records — emails risked landing in spam
  • Each domain had different existing DNS configurations that needed careful handling
  • Client needed a fast turnaround to get their team back to work
The Solution

End-to-end setup, all 9 domains.

01

DNS Audit

Reviewed the existing DNS configurations across all 9 domains in Namecheap to identify what the previous provider had misconfigured or left incomplete.

02

MX Record Configuration

Updated MX records for all 9 domains to point to Google Workspace’s mail servers, ensuring proper email routing priority and delivery.

03

TXT Verification

Added Google-generated TXT verification records to each domain’s DNS zone in Namecheap, then confirmed ownership in the Google Admin console.

04

SPF & DKIM Setup

Configured SPF records to authorize Google’s mail servers and generated DKIM signing keys for each domain to prevent email spoofing and improve deliverability.

05

DMARC Policy

Added DMARC records with monitoring policies to protect against phishing, providing the client with visibility into email authentication results.

06

Testing & Handoff

Verified email send/receive on all 9 domains, confirmed DNS propagation, tested SPF/DKIM/DMARC alignment, and handed off full documentation to the client.
