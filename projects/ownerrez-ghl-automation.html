Overview

Two platforms, one seamless system.

OwnerRez is a powerful property management platform for vacation rentals — but it wasn’t designed to be a full CRM. Guest communication was scattered, follow-ups were manual, and leads from inquiries were getting lost between systems.

This project bridges the gap by connecting OwnerRez to GoHighLevel. Every booking automatically syncs into GHL’s CRM, triggering a complete guest messaging lifecycle — from pre-arrival instructions to post-stay review requests and rebooking offers.

The result is a hands-off system where every guest gets the right message at the right time, and every inquiry is followed up on automatically.

The Challenge

Guest communication was falling apart.

The property manager was juggling OwnerRez for bookings and manually handling all guest communication — sending check-in instructions by hand, forgetting follow-ups, and losing track of inquiries that never converted to bookings.

  • Booking data lived in OwnerRez with no CRM to track guest relationships
  • Check-in instructions sent manually — sometimes late, sometimes forgotten
  • No automated reminders or pre-arrival messaging for upcoming guests
  • Guest inquiries from the website went unanswered for hours or days
  • Zero post-stay follow-up — no review requests, no rebooking offers
  • No visibility into which guests were happy, which had issues, or who to re-engage
The Solution

6 workflows. Every guest, every touchpoint.

01

Booking Sync

OwnerRez reservations automatically sync into GoHighLevel via webhook — creating a contact with all booking details, source tags, and pipeline placement.

02

Guest Intake

Each synced guest gets auto-tagged by source (Airbnb, VRBO, direct), enriched with custom fields, and receives an instant booking confirmation via SMS and email.

03

Pre-Arrival Messaging

Timed sequence at 7 days, 3 days, and 1 day before check-in — delivering local guides, check-in instructions, door codes, and upsell offers.

04

In-Stay Communication

Day-after-arrival check-in message to catch issues early, plus mid-stay recommendations for local dining, activities, and late checkout offers.

05

Post-Stay Follow-up

Thank you message after checkout, review request at 24 hours, gentle reminder at Day 5, and a rebooking offer with 10% discount at Day 14.

06

Lead Capture & Nurture

Website inquiries that don’t convert get instant auto-replies, a 3-day follow-up sequence, and seasonal promo drip to convert them later.
