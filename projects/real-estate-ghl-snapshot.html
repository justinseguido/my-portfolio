The Challenge

Agents were losing leads to slow follow-up and disconnected tools.

Most real estate agents piece together their marketing with a mix of different platforms — one for the website, one for email, another for SMS, and a separate CRM. The result is a fragmented system where leads fall through the cracks and follow-up is slow or non-existent.

  • No unified system for capturing seller and buyer leads separately
  • Slow or manual lead follow-up losing deals to faster-responding competitors
  • Multiple disconnected tools with no central CRM or pipeline
  • No automated nurture sequences to keep leads warm over time
  • Landing pages that look good but don't convert visitors into contacts
  • Zero visibility into which leads are hot and ready to act
The Solution

One snapshot. Everything connected.

Built a high-converting landing page with a clear value proposition, trust signals, and a lead capture form that segments visitors into seller or buyer pipelines automatically.
